My Achievement

The First Step Towards My Dream

scroll

Background

I have always dreamed of being a creator since I was young.

The animations I watched on TV amazed me—their stories captivated me, and
I longed to be part of that world, to create something just as wonderful.

To pursue this dream, I began learning how to draw and how to animate. I practiced with short animations, developed my own original characters and stories. Each step brought me closer to the vision I had as a child.

Now, I have completed my very first full animation project.

Setting

This story tells of a girl with albinism who is disliked by her parents and favors her younger sister. No matter what she does, her parents will not like her, so she develops dissociative identity disorder and identifies the following personalities.

Ren Zhaoti

The dominant personality, responsible for daily life, including going to school, socializing, and interacting with parents. She is cheerful but somewhat forgetful.

Brande White

The protector personality, emerges when the body is in danger and personal safety is threatened. He is gentle in nature, but can be impulsive at times.

Nignolia White

The trauma sufferer bears the trauma, such as conflicts with parents, painful emotions, etc. She is a restrained and rational person who contemplates suicide.

Impact

This animated work has achieved tremendous success, accumulating over 350,000 views on Bilibili to date. This is an affirmation of my hard work and my greatest reward. This success has also strengthened my resolve as a creator, and I will continue to move forward, pursuing my dreams.
